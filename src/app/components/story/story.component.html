Our Story

Discover the journey of love that brought us together. From the first encounter to this moment, explore the chapters of our story, filled with laughter, love, and unforgettable memories

2017

We Met

Alvin, a tech support, and Kay, a junior accountant at FOPM, met. Alvin requested Kay's Facebook, which wasn't accepted (or perhaps forgotten). Alvin invited Kay for a coffee date, marking the beginning of their love story. They shared late-night coffee at Starbucks

January 30, 2021

We Became Official

Alvin and Kay officially became boyfriend and girlfriend, solidifying their relationship after years of getting to know each other and growing closer.

July 8, 2023

He Proposed

With the blessing of their parents and in the presence of close friends, Alvin proposed to Kay, asking her to marry him. It was a moment filled with joy and anticipation as they embarked on the next chapter of their lives together.
